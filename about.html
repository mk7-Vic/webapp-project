About Maina & Sons

Our Story

Maina & Sons Car Hire was founded in Kenya with a simple, unwavering goal: to bridge the gap between complex travel needs and reliable, affordable, and comfortable transportation solutions. We understand that whether you are traveling for business, pleasure, or necessity, the quality of your commute defines your experience.

Over the years, we have evolved from a modest operation into a trusted name in the Kenyan transport sector. By strategically expanding our diverse fleet, ranging from fuel-efficient city commuters to rugged SUVs, and continuously refining our personalized service, we have successfully met the growing demands of international tourists, corporate enterprises, and local travelers alike. Today, we remain committed to our founding principles, ensuring that every kilometer you travel with us is safe, seamless and stress-free.
