Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Booking Information (Pickup/Return dates, locations)
  • Driver's Licence Details (when required for verification)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage your vehicle bookings.
  • To contact you regarding your reservations.
  • To improve our website services and fleet offerings.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and never sell your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Payment providers for secure transaction processing.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences, improve site performance, and analyze website traffic to better serve our customers.

5. Data Security

We protect your customer information through reasonable security measures, including secure encryption for data transmission and restricted access to personal records.

6. Customer Rights

You have the right to request access to your data, correct any inaccurate information, and request the deletion of your personal records where applicable.
