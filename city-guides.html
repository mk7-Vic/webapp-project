    Our comprehensive guides for driving across Kenya's key cities.

    Nairobi Mombasa Kisumu Nakuru

    Nairobi

    Best for City Business & Safari Starts
    Branch Location

    Westlands, Nairobi.

    Proximity to Airport

    Only 30 minutes from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA).

    Popular Attractions

    Nairobi National Park, Giraffe Centre, David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust.

    Best Cars to Hire

    Toyota Axio for city meetings, Toyota Prado for safari expeditions.

    Driving Tips

    Traffic is heavy during peak hours (7-9 AM, 5-7 PM). Plan your airport transfers well in advance.

    Book from Nairobi

    Mombasa

    Best for Coastal Getaways & Leisure
    Branch Location

    Mombasa Town, near Digo Road.

    Proximity to Airport

    25 minutes from Moi International Airport (MBA).

    Popular Attractions

    Fort Jesus, Old Town, Diani Beach (short drive).

    Best Cars to Hire

    luxury sedans or SUVs for scenic coastal drives.

    Driving Tips

    High humidity and heat; ensure your AC is working perfectly. Watch out for Tuk-tuks in town.

    Book from Mombasa

    Kisumu

    Best for Lakeside Trips & Cultural Tours
    Branch Location

    Oginga Odinga Street, Kisumu City.

    Proximity to Airport

    20 minutes from Kisumu International Airport (KIS).

    Popular Attractions

    Dunga Beach, Impala Sanctuary, Kit Mikayi.

    Best Cars to Hire

    Reliable sedans for smooth city and lakeside roads.

    Driving Tips

    Roads are generally less congested than Nairobi, but be cautious of cyclists and motorbikes in the outskirts.

    Book from Kisumu

    Nakuru

    Best for Rift Valley Exploration
    Branch Location

    Kenyatta Avenue, Nakuru Town.

    Proximity to Airport

    Accessible via 2.5-hour drive from Nairobi's JKIA.

    Popular Attractions

    Lake Nakuru National Park, Menengai Crater.

    Best Cars to Hire

    4WD SUVs are highly recommended for park visits and rugged terrain.

    Driving Tips

    Fog can reduce visibility in the early mornings on the highway; always drive with your lights on.
