By booking or hiring a vehicle from Maina & Sons Car Hire, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions.

1. Eligibility

Drivers must be at least 23 years old and possess a valid driving licence with a minimum of two years' driving experience.

2. Reservations

Reservations are subject to vehicle availability. Confirmation is only complete once payment or deposit requirements have been fulfilled.

3. Payments & Deposits

Rental charges must be settled before vehicle collection unless otherwise agreed. Security deposits may be required depending on the vehicle selected and are fully refundable upon safe return of the vehicle.

4. Vehicle Usage

  • Vehicles must only be driven by authorised drivers.
  • Vehicles must not be used for illegal activities.
  • No racing, reckless driving or off-road use unless authorised.
  • Customers are responsible for traffic fines during the rental period.

5. Mileage Policy

Our standard rental rates include a daily mileage allowance of 150km. Excess mileage will be charged at Ksh 30 per kilometer. Unlimited mileage packages are available upon request for long-term rentals.

6. Fuel Policy

Vehicles should be returned with the same fuel level as collected. Additional fuel replacement and refuelling service charges may apply otherwise.

7. Damage & Insurance

All vehicles are comprehensively insured. Customers should inspect vehicles before collection and report any existing damage. New damage incurred during the rental period may result in repair charges or excess fees. Customers remain fully liable for damages resulting from gross negligence, driving under the influence, misuse, or breach of these terms.

8. Cancellations & Late Returns

Bookings may be cancelled before the scheduled pickup time. Refunds depend on the notice period and payment terms. Vehicles must be returned at the agreed-upon time and location. Late returns without prior notification and approval may incur additional hourly penalty fees or an extra full-day charge.

9. Traffic Violations & Fines

The renter is fully responsible for any parking tickets, speeding fines, toll charges, or traffic violations incurred during the rental period. An administrative processing fee may be applied to the renter's account for handling these fines.

10. Cross-Border Travel

Vehicles are strictly intended for use within the borders of Kenya. Cross-border travel is prohibited unless prior written consent is obtained from Maina & Sons Car Hire, which may require additional insurance coverage and clearance documents.

11. Lost Keys & Lockouts

The renter is responsible for the safekeeping of the vehicle's keys. In the event of lost keys, damaged fobs, or locking the keys inside the vehicle, the renter will be liable for the cost of replacement, reprogramming, and any associated recovery services.

12. Smoking & Pets

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside all Maina & Sons Car Hire vehicles. A penalty fee will be charged for detailing if evidence of smoking is found. Pets are only allowed in designated pet-friendly vehicles and must be transported in carriers. The vehicle must be returned vacuumed and free of pet hair.

13. Cleaning & Personal Belongings

We take pride in providing clean and sanitized vehicles. If a vehicle is returned excessively dirty or stained, a professional detailing and valet fee will be deducted from the security deposit. Maina & Sons Car Hire accepts no responsibility for loss or damage to personal property left in the vehicle at any time.

14. Breakdowns & Roadside Assistance

In the event of a mechanical breakdown, contact our 24/7 support team immediately. Do not attempt to repair the vehicle or engage third-party mechanics without our explicit authorization. We provide roadside assistance and replacement vehicles subject to availability and the nature of the breakdown.

15. Governing Law

These Terms of Service and any separate agreements whereby we provide you services shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the Laws of Kenya. Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Kenyan courts.

16. Contact

For questions regarding these Terms of Service, kindly contact our customer support team before making a reservation.
